# Build Launch — Plain Language (Average Joe)

**Audience:** Anyone who wants a simple, jargon-free picture of what we’re building and launching.

---

## What we’re doing

We’re **building** a platform that gives attorneys and businesses the power of a full team through AI — and we’re **launching** it in a clear, repeatable way so that the website, the “talk to us” voice agent, and the product all go live together and stay on-message. We call that whole process **Build Launch**.

---

## What’s in it for you

- **One place to learn** — Build Launch is the master set of instructions and steps. Executives get a one-pager; developers get full technical docs; you get this plain-language guide.
- **Voice agent (“Talk to Alex”)** — You can talk to our AI voice agent instead of filling out forms. Alex explains the product, answers common questions, and connects you to a human when you need one. Build Launch makes sure Alex’s answers and behavior are consistent and safe.
- **Website and marketing** — When we launch, the website, blog, and “talk to Alex” link all go live in a set order. Build Launch defines that order and what goes on each page so nothing is missing or wrong.

---

## What the voice agent does

- **Conversations** — Alex greets you, asks what you’re interested in, and answers in plain English (e.g. how the 60-minute onboarding works, how we compare to big firms, pricing, security).
- **Troubleshooting** — If something’s unclear or you’re stuck, Alex has instructions to clarify or offer to have a human follow up.
- **Escalation** — For pricing, legal questions, or “I want to talk to someone,” Alex hands off to the team (e.g. email rbailey@connectedagents.ai or “we’ll have someone reach out”).

You don’t need to know any tech — you just talk. Build Launch is what we use to make sure that experience is the same every time and that we don’t promise things we shouldn’t (e.g. legal advice).
