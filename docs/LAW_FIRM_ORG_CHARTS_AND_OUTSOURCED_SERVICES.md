# Law Firm Org Charts — Multinational, Mid-Size, Small & Solo (with Assistant Who Outsources)

**Purpose:** Define **organizational structure at every level** and **all outsourced services** for four law firm models: (1) multinational, (2) mid-size, (3) small, (4) solo with an assistant who outsources. Use for staffing, RACI, vendor selection, and aligning with litigation/platform workflows (e.g. LitigationForce, OneWish OS).

**Governance:** [constitution.md](constitution.md) (human-in-the-loop, claim typing); [PALANTIR_TYPE_DIGITAL_TRANSFORMATION_TEAM_ORG_AND_ARCHITECTURE.md](PALANTIR_TYPE_DIGITAL_TRANSFORMATION_TEAM_ORG_AND_ARCHITECTURE.md) (enterprise/client org); ABA/EDRM/Sedona where applicable.

| Level | Role | Reports to | Scope | Key responsibilities |
|-------|------|------------|-------|----------------------|

| **Firm** | Equity Partners | Managing Partner | Ownership; practice areas | Client work; BD; associate supervision; profit share |
| **Firm** | Office Manager / Admin | Managing Partner | Office operations, admin staff | Office ops; reception; supplies; vendor liaison; may do billing support |

### 3.2 Lawyer level (tier 2)

| Level | Role | Reports to | Scope |
|-------|------|------------|-------|
| **Lawyer** | Non-Equity Partner (optional) | Managing Partner | Senior attorney; salary + bonus |
| **Lawyer** | Counsel (optional) | Partner(s) | Experienced; specialist or flex role |
| **Lawyer** | Associate(s) | Partner(s) | Research; drafting; court; client work under supervision |

Small firms may have no formal practice groups; partners often cover multiple areas. Associates and support report to assigned partner(s).

## 4. Solo law firm with assistant who outsources — org chart (every level)

### 4.1 Structure

```
                    ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
                    │  SOLO ATTORNEY (owner, only lawyer)                              │
                    │  All legal work; client relationships; final sign-off             │
                    └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
                                                          │
    ┌──────────────────────┬──────────────────────┬───────┴───────┐
    │                      │                      │               │
    ▼                      ▼                      ▼               ▼
┌─────────────┐    ┌─────────────┐    ┌─────────────────┐  ┌─────────────┐
│ In-house    │    │ Outsourced  │    │ Outsourced      │  │ Outsourced  │
│ Assistant   │    │ Legal /     │    │ Admin /         │  │ Specialty   │
│ (1)         │    │ Paralegal   │    │ Operations      │  │ (e.g. tax,  │
│             │    │ / VA        │    │                 │  │  IP, etc.)  │
└─────────────┘    └─────────────┘    └─────────────────┘  └─────────────┘
```

| Level | Role | Reports to | Scope | Key responsibilities |
|-------|------|------------|-------|----------------------|
| **Solo** | Solo Attorney | — (owner) | All legal and business | Client work; court; advice; filings; BD; vendor selection; final sign-off |
| **Solo** | In-house Assistant (1) | Solo Attorney | Admin, intake, scheduling, coordination with outsourced | Intake; scheduling; correspondence; billing support; **coordinates and manages outsourced vendors**; document handling; client communication |
| **Solo** | Outsourced legal/paralegal/VA | Solo Attorney (often via Assistant) | Legal support, paralegal, virtual assistant | Per outsourced services list below; contract/virtual paralegal, research, doc prep |
| **Solo** | Outsourced admin/operations | Solo Attorney (often via Assistant) | Bookkeeping, IT, marketing | Billing; bookkeeping; IT; marketing; per list below |
| **Solo** | Outsourced specialty (as needed) | Solo Attorney | Tax, IP, etc. | Of counsel or contract specialists per matter |

### 4.2 Assistant’s role in outsourcing

The **in-house assistant** typically:

- **Selects and coordinates** outsourced vendors (paralegal, research, court reporting, eDiscovery, etc.).
- **Scopes work** and sends tasks to vendors; **tracks deadlines** and **quality**.
- **Receives deliverables**; organizes for attorney review; **flags privilege/confidentiality**.
- **Manages contracts and billing** with vendors (or works with outsourced bookkeeping).
- **Escalates** to the solo attorney for legal decisions, conflict checks, and sign-off.
